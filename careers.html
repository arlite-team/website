Our Positions at Arlite Research

We're always looking for ambitious and talented people to join our team. Below are the positions that we're currently actively hiring for, including our Analytical Researcher, Quantitative Engineer, and Management Consultant Positions.

Don't see a good fit, but still want to be part of the team? Let us know what you'd like to do at jobs@arliteresearch.com.

Analytical Researcher

In this role, you'll work with a team of researchers to conduct extensive research into various parts of the market. You'll use analytics tools like Tableau and PowerBI to aid you. You'll write research reports based on clients' needs.

Quantitative Engineer

In this role, you'll work with a team to write mathematical models and machine learning algorithms to help clients achieve their goals, including forecasting, risk analyses, return rates, classification, and regression.

Management Consultant

In this role, you'll work directly with clients' management teams to provide guidance in their strategies and resolve their headwinds. You'll work alongside partners and researchers to create agile solutions for our clients.
